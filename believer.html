Sycamore Church

We are a member of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) within the United Kingdom and Northern Ireland. A church for everyone.



Believer

Age: 17 years and above

This group is for those who want to strengthen their Faith in God, people that want to learn more God, deeper understanding about the new ways and walks of salvation. It is a requisite for anyone who want to be an active volunteers, ministers or just joining the ministry (church) in order for us to have same understanding about the scripture, God and our christian lives. It is also an encouraging class for those that just give their life to Christ.


  • Strong biblical foundation about the person of Trinity
  • Dynamics of Faith, Salvation and Spiritual walks
  • Deep understanding about christian life, discipline and conduct etc.
