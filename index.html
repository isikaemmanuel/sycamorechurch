Who We Are


Sycamore Church is a member of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) within the United Kingdom and Northern Ireland. A church for everyone.

We are a family church and so we encourage parents to let their children join us to fellowship at a special section for children. Our top priority is to seek and know Jesus, to pattern our lives according to His. We look forward to welcome you to our services. God bless you richly.

Our Mandate

To Make Heaven. To Take as Many People as Possible with Us To Have A Member of RCCG In Every Family of All Nations To Accomplish No. 1 Above, Holiness Will Be Our Lifestyle To Accomplish Nos. 2 & 3 Above, We Will Plant Churches Within Five Minutes Walking Distance in Every City and Town of Developing Countries and Within Five Minutes Driving Distance in Every City and Town of Developed Countries We Will Pursue These Objectives Until Every Nation in the World Is Reached for Jesus Christ Our Lord.


Our Belief and Priority

Our Priority is to seek and know Jesus in order to pattern our lives to His. To equip, produce and experience We believe in God, Jesus and Holy Spirit (The Trinity), Repentance unto God, Fruitfulness, Baptism of Water and Holy Spirit, Divine Healing, Holy Matrimony, Church of God, Prophesy in the Church, Holy Communion or The Lord's supper, The Second coming of Christ, New heaven and new earth, Death and Resurrection of Jesus.
